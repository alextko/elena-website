Healthcare costs shouldn't be a mystery.

Since 2021, federal law requires hospitals and insurers to publish their actual negotiated prices. The problem? That data is buried in massive spreadsheets no one can read.

Elena pulls from these CMS-mandated price transparency files, compares costs across providers in your area, and shows you what you'll actually pay — before you book.
